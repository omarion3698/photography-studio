ABOUT US

Who are we?

Photography Studio is a Kenyan based photo studio and creative agency specializing in photography, video production, and creative use of digital technology. We collaborate with Not-for-profit organizations, education institutions, hotels, and small businesses to create distinctive brands that are both thoughtful and beautiful. Photography Studio opened in June 2019, with a dream to Create, Capture and Captivate.Photography Studio was born out of the desire to achieve and provide high quality services in an under performing market.

What we do?

We offer a full-service location & photography studio, print shop and creative company specializing in people and event photography, video coverage, digital printing and gift shop. We focus on corporate and private events, commercial projects, weddings, digital printing and creative design.

How we do it!

Photography Studio utilizes state of the art photographic equipment and top range printing instruments with highly developed techniques to offer premium quality professional results, accompanied by our renowned tailored services. In 2020, we introduced sublimation to add aesthetic value to photos by providing clients with unique, personalised items with their own memories attached to each gift. Photography Studio has gained vast experience in offering services to create a force perfect for putting your business needs in the front line of the marketing world. Our secret at Photography is simple: we believe in providing the BEST photography, video coverage & quality digital prints with a flair for creative design combined with stellar customer service.With us, quality and reliability is guaranteed.

Create, Capture, Captivate.

Our mission is to combine technical proficiency with aesthetic intuition to produce remarkable solutions for our clients.We welcome collaboration with our clients to define and achieve the desired look and feeling for their photographs, videos and prints.Our broad capabilities and diverse range of style has allowed us to provide for a wide array of clientele,ranging from private individuals to public corporations.
